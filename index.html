What we do

Moens custom woodworks is a custom woodworking shop located in central Wisconsin. Creating custom furniture, cabinetry and performing custom on site carpentry is our specialty. There is no project to big or small and our experience includes but is not limited to:

Commercial: Signage/fixtures and remodels

Residential: Large pieces such as; kitchen cabinets, vanities, built-in’s, tables, chairs, dressers, bed frames/headboards, night stands, chests, crown molding and doors to smaller pieces that include; cutting boards, cribbage boards, coasters, urns, and jewelry boxes.

Custom projects are our passion so any idea/project is welcome!

Our Process

Meet

The first step to creating your custom piece is by establishing a line of communication so we can understand your vision, style and expectations of what you are looking to achieve. Weather you have a large project or a small commission Our process is fully customizable which means communication is key: setting up a meeting at our shop, your home or business as well as virtual meeting options will be the first step.

Design

Designing and creating a plan is our next step in the process; getting ideas down on paper to help visualize the end goal helps ensure a successful project. Whether you are starting from scratch or come to us with a design in mind; we will create a plan together to help obtain a cost estimate and time-line to bring your vision to reality!

Build

Once our preparation is complete, it’s time to build! Regardless of how extensive the project my be we make sure to tailer each step of the build to be efficient and precise. Using highest of quality materials and finely tuned machines to create the best joinery and craftsmanship for the application. We take immense pride in our work and attention to detail to ensure that each piece or project will last for generations.

Finish

The finishing stage is yet another custom step ensuring we use the best product for the job to provide longevity. Whether it’s a custom stain, paint, or letting the natural beauty of the wood shine through; we will choose the correct finish to accomplish your project goals.

Delivery/Install

After the build and finish process is complete, we will deliver and install your custom piece in its exact place. Whether it is a free standing piece of furniture or custom cabinets that require installation. Making the job complete from start to finish to ensure your satisfaction.
